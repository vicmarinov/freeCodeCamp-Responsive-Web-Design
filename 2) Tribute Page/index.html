Steve Jobs: A Visionary Who Changed the World

Steve Jobs was a visionary entrepreneur and the co-founder of Apple Inc. He revolutionized personal computing, music, phones, tablet computing, and digital publishing. His relentless drive for innovation and perfection made Apple one of the world's most valuable companies. From the creation of the Macintosh to the iPod, iPhone, and iPad, Jobs transformed industries and touched millions of lives.
